Description

Lasagna is a very popular dish.

Ingredients:

  • Pasta base
  • Tomato sauce
  • 1 pound of ground beef
  • Feta cheese

How to prepare:

  1. Cook the ground beef
  2. Soak pasta base in boiling water
  3. Set a floor of pasta base, and put meat and feta cheese
  4. Repeat the previous step until the container is full
