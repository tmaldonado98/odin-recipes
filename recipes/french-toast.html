Description

French toast is a great option for a morning breakfast.

Ingredients:

  • Four bread squares
  • Three eggs
  • Milk
  • (Optional) Syrup

How to prepare:

  1. Mix the eggs with milk in a bowl
  2. Dip your toast in the mix
  3. Set your toast on the frying pan and cook until firm
  4. Remove from frying pan, add syrup (optional), and enjoy
